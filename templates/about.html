About our project:

After using PCA to consolidate the dimensionality of our data set,

we were ready to explore the data and make some visualizations.

Using outcomes of COVID cases and deaths, we looked at the individual

distributions of these variables across different states, as well as

the breakdown between COVID cases and deaths by state.

***

We also looked at the impacts of other factors on deaths and cases, such

as available ICU beds and vaccines administered in each state.

________________________________________

How to use this app:

Click on the "Vis" tabs to view each visualization.

Our visualizations are interactive!

Click on the features on each graph to play with the data!
